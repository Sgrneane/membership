Membership Guidelines

At present, there are three individual membership and one institutional membership categories in Nepal Geotechnical Society:

  1. Regular (General) Member
  2. Life Member
  3. Student Member
  4. Institutional Member (Corporate Member)

The following is the membership fee structure:

S.No. Membership Type Fee Details
01 Regular (General) Membership First time registration fee: NRs. 2,000 and annual renewal fee: NRs. 1,000
02 Life Membership egistration fee: NRs. 10,000
03 Student Membership First time registration fee: NRs. 1,000 and annual renewal fee: NRs. 500
04 Institutional Membership First time registration fee: NRs. 1,00,000 and annual renewal fee: NRs. 10,000
